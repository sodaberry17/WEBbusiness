History

Lee Hee Seung, commonly known as Heeseung, is a South Korean singer and member of the boy group ENHYPEN, formed through the reality survival show "I-LAND" in 2020. Born on October 15, 2001, in Namyangju, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea, Heeseung demonstrated musical talent from a young age, excelling in both vocals and dance. Heeseung trained under BigHit Entertainment, now known as HYBE Corporation, where he honed his skills before competing on "I-LAND." His performance on the show was notable for its consistency and versatility, earning him a place in the final lineup of ENHYPEN, which debuted on November 30, 2020, with their EP "BORDER: DAY ONE." As the main vocalist and one of the lead dancers of ENHYPEN, Heeseung has contributed significantly to the group's success. ENHYPEN quickly gained popularity both domestically and internationally, with their music resonating with a global audience. The group is known for their powerful performances and cohesive teamwork, attributes to which Heeseung's skills and leadership have been crucial. Heeseung continues to grow as an artist, participating actively in songwriting and production. His journey from a trainee to a celebrated idol highlights his dedication and passion for music, making him a prominent figure in the K-pop industry.
